Explanation :

A can do a piece of work in 10 days, and B can do the same work in 20 days. With the help of C, they finished the work in 4 days?

Similarly, B can do 100/12 = 8.233% of work in a day.

Therefore, in a single day both can do 10% + 8.233% = 18.233% of work in a day.

2 days -> 2*18.33% = 36.66%

3 days -> 3*18.33% = 54.96%

4 days -> 4*18.33% = 73.32%
